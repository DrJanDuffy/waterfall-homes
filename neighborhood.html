About Waterfall

Waterfall is a premier gated community located in the heart of North Las Vegas, Nevada. Established in 2005, this well-maintained neighborhood offers spacious homes with modern layouts, ranging from 1,285 to 1,991 square feet.

The community is known for its resort-style amenities, including a swimming pool, spa, fitness center, pickleball courts, and scenic walking trails. Residents enjoy a strong sense of community with regular social events and well-maintained common areas.

Located in the 89085 ZIP code, Waterfall offers convenient access to the 215 Beltway, making it easy to commute to the Las Vegas Strip, Downtown, Henderson, and Summerlin. The neighborhood is served by highly-rated schools in the Clark County School District.

With competitive home prices starting in the mid $300,000s and low HOA fees of just $50-80 per month, Waterfall offers exceptional value for families, professionals, and retirees alike.
