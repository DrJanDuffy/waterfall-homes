Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices is synonymous with integrity, financial strength, and exceptional service—values that Dr. Duffy embodies in her daily practice. This affiliation provides her clients with access to a global network of over 50,000 real estate professionals, cutting-edge marketing tools, and resources that help ensure successful outcomes whether buying or selling a home.

A Commitment to the Waterfall Community

Dr. Duffy's specialization in the Waterfall neighborhood is not merely professional—it's personal. She has witnessed the community grow and evolve since its establishment in 2005, and she understands the lifestyle, amenities, and value that Waterfall offers to its residents. This deep familiarity allows her to provide insights that go beyond basic property information, helping clients understand what it truly means to live in this premier gated community.

The Waterfall neighborhood offers homes ranging from 1,285 to 1,991 square feet, with prices typically ranging from the mid $300,000s to the mid $500,000s. Dr. Duffy's expertise in this specific market segment means she can accurately price homes for sellers to maximize their return while helping buyers identify properties that offer the best value for their investment. Her knowledge extends to the community's resort-style amenities, including the swimming pool, spa, fitness center, pickleball courts, and walking trails that make Waterfall such an attractive place to call home.

Whether working with first-time homebuyers navigating the process for the first time or experienced investors looking to expand their portfolios, Dr. Duffy provides the same level of dedication, expertise, and attention to detail.
