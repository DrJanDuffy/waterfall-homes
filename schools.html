Clark County School District

The Waterfall neighborhood is served by the Clark County School District (CCSD), the fifth-largest school district in the United States. CCSD provides comprehensive educational programs from pre-kindergarten through 12th grade.

In addition to the zoned public schools, families in Waterfall have access to numerous charter schools, magnet programs, and private school options throughout the Las Vegas Valley.

The district offers various specialized programs including GATE (Gifted and Talented Education), career and technical education, and advanced placement courses at the high school level.

School Choice Options

Nevada's school choice programs allow families to explore educational options beyond their zoned schools, including:

  • Charter schools with specialized curricula
  • Magnet schools and programs
  • Private and parochial schools
  • Homeschool support programs
