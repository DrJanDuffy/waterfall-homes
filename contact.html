Why Contact Dr. Jan Duffy?

When you reach out to Dr. Jan Duffy, you're connecting with a dedicated real estate professional who has made the Waterfall neighborhood her specialty. With over seventeen years of experience in the Las Vegas Valley real estate market and deep expertise in the Waterfall community, Dr. Duffy provides the knowledge, guidance, and personalized service that makes a real difference in your real estate experience.

Unlike agents who cover broad geographic areas and treat all neighborhoods the same, Dr. Duffy has focused her practice on Waterfall and the surrounding North Las Vegas area. This specialization means she understands the nuances of the local market—which streets are most desirable, which floor plans are most popular, what buyers are looking for, and how to price homes for maximum results. This expertise translates into better outcomes for her clients, whether buying or selling.

What to Expect When You Contact Dr. Duffy

Dr. Duffy is committed to responsive, professional communication. When you reach out, you can expect a prompt response—typically within two hours during her available hours. She understands that real estate decisions often involve time-sensitive opportunities and emotional considerations, and she prioritizes being available when her clients need her.

Your initial conversation with Dr. Duffy will focus on understanding your needs and goals. Whether you're looking to buy your first home, sell your current property, or simply explore your options, she'll take the time to listen and provide honest, helpful guidance. There's no pressure and no obligation—just straightforward information to help you make informed decisions.

For Buyers

If you're interested in buying a home in Waterfall, Dr. Duffy will discuss your needs, preferences, and budget to understand what you're looking for. She'll explain the current market conditions, walk you through the buying process, and answer any questions you have. If you're ready to start looking at homes, she can set up a property search and schedule showings at your convenience.

For Sellers

If you're considering selling your Waterfall home, Dr. Duffy will provide a free, no-obligation market analysis to help you understand your home's current value. She'll discuss her marketing approach, explain the selling process, and answer questions about timing, pricing, and what to expect. Her goal is to give you the information you need to make confident decisions about selling your property.

Communication Preferences

Dr. Duffy understands that everyone has different communication preferences, and she's happy to accommodate yours. Whether you prefer phone calls, text messages, emails, or in-person meetings, she'll work with your preferred method to ensure smooth, convenient communication throughout your real estate journey.

For urgent matters or when you need an immediate response, calling directly at (702) 842-2276 is typically the fastest option. For detailed questions or when you want to provide information in writing, email works well. And for scheduling appointments, the online booking system through Calendly offers convenient self-service scheduling.

Frequently Asked Questions About Contacting Dr. Duffy

What are Dr. Duffy's hours of availability?

Dr. Duffy is available seven days a week from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM. She understands that real estate decisions don't always happen during traditional business hours and makes herself available to accommodate her clients' schedules, including evenings and weekends.

How quickly will Dr. Duffy respond to my inquiry?

Dr. Duffy strives to respond to all inquiries within two hours during her available hours. For urgent matters, calling directly at (702) 842-2276 is recommended for the fastest response. Messages received outside of available hours will be returned first thing the following morning.

Is there any cost or obligation to contact Dr. Duffy?

No, there is absolutely no cost or obligation to contact Dr. Duffy. Initial consultations, market analyses for sellers, and general real estate guidance are provided free of charge. She believes in building relationships based on trust and providing value before any formal engagement.

Can I schedule a home tour for evenings or weekends?

Yes, Dr. Duffy offers flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends. She understands that many clients work during traditional business hours and need to view properties during their off-hours. Use the online scheduling system or call directly to arrange a convenient time.

What information should I have ready when I contact Dr. Duffy?

While no preparation is required, it's helpful to have a general idea of your timeline, budget (for buyers), and goals. For sellers, knowing your approximate mortgage balance and any timeline constraints is useful. However, Dr. Duffy is happy to help you work through these details during your conversation.

Does Dr. Duffy work with first-time homebuyers?

Absolutely! Dr. Duffy enjoys working with first-time homebuyers and provides additional guidance to help them navigate the process with confidence. She takes time to explain each step, answer questions thoroughly, and ensure first-time buyers feel comfortable and informed throughout their journey.
